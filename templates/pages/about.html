About MMU Portal

Our Mission

MMU Portal is designed to facilitate knowledge sharing among students and educators. Our mission is to create a collaborative environment where educational resources can be easily accessed and shared.

What We Offer

  • A centralized platform for note sharing
  • Easy upload and download of study materials
  • Recognition for quality contributions through our Hall of Fame
  • Secure access to educational resources

How It Works

  1. Request access to the portal
  2. Once approved, login with your credentials
  3. Upload your notes to share with others
  4. Download notes shared by your peers
  5. Earn recognition for your quality contributions
